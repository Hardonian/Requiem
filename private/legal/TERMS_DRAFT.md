## 1. Description of Service

Requiem provides a Provable AI Runtime (the "Service") designed to facilitate deterministic execution and governance of AI agents. The Service includes the `reach` Command Line Interface, the ReadyLayer management dashboard, and associated APIs.

## 2. Integrity & Determinism Claims

While Requiem is designed to provide bit-perfect determinism and cryptographic receipts, the Customer acknowledges that:
- **Upstream Providers**: Changes in underlying model providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) may cause semantic drift that Requiem detects but cannot control.
- **Environment Variance**: Extreme hardware deviations (e.g., varying CPU instruction sets) may impact specific low-level fingerprints.
