# Acceptable Use Policy (Draft): Requiem

**Version**: 0.1.0-DRAFT  
**Last Updated**: 2026-03-02

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the prohibited uses of the Requiem Provable AI Runtime. This is to ensure the integrity of the platform and prevent misuse of autonomous agents.

## 2. Prohibited Activities

Users of Requiem shall not utilize the service for:

- **Illegal Acts**: Any activity that violates local, state, national, or international law.
- **Harmful Content**: Generating or executing agents that create malware, hate speech, or promote violence.
- **Unauthorized Access**: Attempting to bypass the Policy VM or escape the native sandbox to access host systems.
- **Fraud & Deception**: Using agents to impersonate individuals or entities for fraudulent purposes.
- **Network Attacks**: Using tool calls to perform DDoS attacks, port scanning, or unauthorized network exploitation.

## 3. Enforcement

Requiem reserves the right to suspend "Proof Credits" or terminate access to the ReadyLayer control plane if a breach of this policy is detected.

## 4. Reporting Misuse

If you witness or suspect misuse of the Requiem platform, please report it to <abuse@requiem.sh>.
