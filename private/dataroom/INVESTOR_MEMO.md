## 1. The Opportunity

Artificial Intelligence is transitioning from "Generation" (Chat) to "Action" (Agents). However, the infrastructure for autonomous action is broken. Companies cannot trust AI with high-stakes decisions because they lack a **Provable Runtime**. Requiem is the first platform to provide bit-perfect, deterministic execution with cryptographic receipts for every AI decision.

## 2. Solving the "Integrity Gap"

Current AI stacks rely on probabilistic filters and logs. If an AI agent executes an unauthorized trade or deletes data, there is no immutable proof of *how* or *why*. Requiem fills this gap by treating AI execution as a semantic state machine, ensuring governance is enforced at the runtime level.
