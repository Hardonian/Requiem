# Packaging & Pricing Model: Requiem

**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Last Updated**: 2026-03-02

## 1. Pricing Philosophy

Requiem pricing is based on **Trust Integrity Metrics**. We charge for the *verification* and *governance* of executions, not just the raw compute.

## 2. Subscription Tiers

| Feature | **OSS** (Community) | **Pro** (Growth) | **Enterprise** (Scale) |

## 3. Metering Units (The "Proof Credit")

A "Proof Credit" covers:

- 1x Policy Evaluation
- 1x BLAKE3 Fingerprint Generation
- 1x Entry into the Semantic Ledger
- Storage of execution metadata

*Note: Model token costs are pass-through or handled via the customer's own API keys.*

## 4. Add-on Modules

### Compliance Pack (Enterprise Only)

- Automated SOC 2 mapping for AI runs.
- HIPAA-compliant data masking at the Policy Gate.
- Custom BAA (Business Associate Agreement).

### Cluster Governance

- Sync execution receipts across global regions.
- Leaderboard for "Proven Determinism" scores across teams.

## 5. Discounting Logic

- **Academic/Non-Profit**: 70% discount on Pro tier.
- **Enterprise Volume**: Annual commitments over $50k include 20% credit bonus.
