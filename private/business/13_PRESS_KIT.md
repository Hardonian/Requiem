# Press Kit: Requiem

## 1. Product Description (Boilerplate)

Requiem is the first Provable AI Runtime, providing a deterministic execution layer for autonomous agents. By creating a cryptographic receipt for every AI decision, Requiem enables enterprises to deploy AI with unprecedented trust, transparency, and auditability. Requiem is built on a high-performance native engine and incorporates formal verification to ensure that governance is not just promised, but enforced at the runtime level.
