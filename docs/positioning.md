# Positioning

Requiem is for teams that need deterministic execution and replayable evidence, not only orchestration convenience.

## Best fit

- Engineering teams that need reproducible incident review.
- Operators that require explicit policy-gated execution records.
- Technical evaluators who need command-level verification, not dashboard-only claims.

## Not a fit

- Teams seeking a no-governance prompt playground.
- Workloads that do not need replay/proof boundaries.
- Environments where deterministic constraints are intentionally relaxed.

## Core differentiation (implemented in this repo)

- Determinism and replay checks in CLI/verification scripts.
- Artifact integrity through digest-addressed storage patterns.
- Policy + evidence connection through verification commands.
